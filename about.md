[Ruby](http://ruby-lang.org),
[Elixir](http://elixir-lang.org),
[JavaScript](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/JavaScript),
[PostgreSQL](http://postgresql.org), and
[Amazon Web Services](https://aws.amazon.com).
